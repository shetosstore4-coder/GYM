About Gym Tracker

Your Ultimate Fitness Companion

Welcome to Gym Tracker, a unified and minimalist web platform designed to streamline your workout logistics and map your physiological adaptations over macrocycles. This program serves two distinct operational methodologies:

1. Structural Workout Planning

The primary dashboard delivers instantaneous access to structured localized splits for specific microcycles (Sunday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday). Each daily plan breaks down into highly localized training movements matched with animated interactive guidelines to keep your posture, cadence, and mechanics precise.

2. Comprehensive 12-Week Body Analytics

True biological composition change requires micro-adjustments. The dedicated Body navigation hub partitions your year into a clean 12-week analytical grid. By recording your biometrics weekly, you build a data profile monitoring:

  • Total skeletal height and current weight vectors.
  • Adipose tissue calculation (Body Fat Percentage).
  • Active metabolic mass indexation (Muscle & Bone Mass variants).
  • Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) tracking to optimize caloric windows.

All biometric information captured by this system is structured and safely compiled within your browser local runtime context, ensuring permanent data tracking.
